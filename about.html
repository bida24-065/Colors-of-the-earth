About Us

Colours of the Earth is a flower store based in Botswana, created to provide beautifully curated floral arrangements for all of life’s occasions — from joyful weddings and birthdays to moments of sympathy and corporate decor needs.

Our business is guided by the belief that flowers are more than just decoration — they are a form of emotional expression. With this in mind, we strive to deliver not only fresh blooms but also exceptional customer experiences, online and offline. Delivery is available nationwide, bringing our beautiful arrangements straight to your doorstep.

Our website allows customers to:

  • View a variety of floral products across different categories
  • Send inquiries or place orders using an intuitive form
  • Explore seasonal promotions and bouquet inspirations
  • Read tips on flower care to get the most from their purchases
